How We Work

Extending capacity.
Not replacing it.



Veridian is designed to complement internal teams—not compete with them. We bring senior perspective and systems expertise at moments when additional capacity creates outsized impact.



How Veridian Thinks

Systems over screens

We focus on the underlying systems that generate interfaces—not just the interfaces themselves. This means our work compounds: solve the system problem once, and every screen benefits.

Decisions over deliverables

The most valuable design work often isn't the final mockup—it's the clarity that emerges from the process. We optimize for helping leadership teams make confident decisions, not for pixel-perfect artifacts.

Constraints as creative fuel

Real products have real constraints: engineering capacity, technical debt, business timelines. We don't design in a vacuum. We design within your reality—and often find that constraints produce better solutions.



Working With Teams

We collaborate closely with internal stakeholders—each with their own perspective and expertise.

With Designers

We work alongside internal designers as collaborators, not replacements. We bring systems thinking and senior perspective; they bring product context and user understanding.

With Product Managers

We help translate product strategy into design direction. Our role is to surface tradeoffs, clarify options, and support confident decision-making.

With Engineers

We design for real engineering constraints. Early collaboration with technical teams ensures our work is buildable—and often reveals better solutions.

With Leadership

We partner directly with founders, CPOs, and design leaders on strategic questions. Our goal is to help you see around corners and make decisions with conviction.



Engagement Shapes

We structure engagements around outcomes, not hours. The right shape depends on what you're trying to accomplish.

Focused Sprint

Intensive collaboration on a specific problem. Typically 4-8 weeks, with clear deliverables and decision points. Good for design system foundations, UX audits, or strategic design direction.

Embedded Partnership

Ongoing collaboration over months. We become an extension of your team, contributing to roadmap planning and major initiatives. Good for complex products or teams in transition.

Advisory

Lightweight ongoing access for strategic guidance. Regular check-ins, design reviews, and on-demand input. Good for teams with strong internal capability who want senior perspective.



How Pricing Works

We price based on value and scope—not hours.

Hourly billing creates misaligned incentives. It rewards inefficiency and penalizes expertise. We prefer fixed-scope engagements with clear deliverables, or retainer arrangements that provide predictable access.

Every engagement starts with a scoping conversation to understand your situation, define outcomes, and determine fit. From there, we provide a clear proposal with transparent pricing.

We work with a small number of teams each year. If there's mutual fit, we'll find a structure that works for both sides.
