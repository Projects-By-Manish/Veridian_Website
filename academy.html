Program Overview

Thinking-first, practice-driven design foundations.

This bootcamp is for learners who want to enter UI/UX, graphic design, product design, or creative roles with clarity and confidence—not just software shortcuts.

What this program is

An 8-week, cohort-based program designed to build strong design fundamentals and apply them on a guided real-world project inside Veridian Design Studio.

What this program is not

This is not a tool-only course or a job-guarantee program. It is structured for learners who are willing to attend sessions, complete exercises, and accept critique.

Who this is ideal for

Final-year or recent graduates, early-career professionals exploring a transition into design, and learners who want both learning and portfolio-worthy project experience.

Curriculum

4 weeks of structured learning.
4 weeks of real project work.

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–4

Design Foundations (Hybrid)

  • How designers observe problems, frame opportunities, and think visually.
  • Core principles of design thinking, ideation, storytelling, and critique.
  • Fundamentals of typography, color theory, layout, and visual hierarchy.
  • Practical skills using Figma and Illustrator.
  • How to present work, receive feedback, and iterate with clarity.
Week 1

Design Thinking Foundations

Week 2

Typography, Color & Layout

Week 3

Figma & Illustrator Mastery

Week 4

Capstone Mini-Project + Portfolio

Phase 2 · Weeks 5–8

Real Project Internship

  • Guided studio project under Veridian mentors.
  • Understanding real briefs, constraints, and client context.
  • Research, exploration, and concept development.
  • Visual execution, refinement, and feedback loops.
  • Documentation and presentation of work as case studies, not simulations.

Each week includes

Live sessions, practical exercises, daily learning logs, and group critiques with structured feedback.

What You Walk Away With

A clear foundation for advanced design paths.

The goal is not to make you a senior designer in 8 weeks. The goal is to give you the thinking patterns, visual fundamentals, and project exposure to confidently take the next step.

Design Foundations Certificate

Certificate from Veridian Design Studio on successful completion of both phases and capstone requirements.

Portfolio-ready case studies

1–2 case studies from your capstone and real project internship, structured to show process, decisions, and outcomes.

Clarity on next steps

Clear feedback on your strengths, improvement areas, and readiness for advanced UI/UX, Product, or Visual Design programs.

Who This Is For

Early-stage designers who want serious practice.

Ideal for

Final-year or recent graduates from Visual Communication, Fine Arts, Architecture, Engineering, and related disciplines; early professionals exploring a career transition into design; learners who want a mix of learning and guided project experience.

Not ideal for

Those looking only for a job guarantee or shortcuts, unwilling to attend sessions or accept critique, or designers with 3+ years of professional experience.
